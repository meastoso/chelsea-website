Chelsea Glenn

Highly praised for her marvelous creations with vibrant balloon animals and imaginative facepaint, Chelsea lives and works at the jersey shore. With 7+ Years of experience working as an entertainer, Chelsea can be found at local events and children’s parties. When Chelsea isn’t twisting up colorful shapes, she is still creating! Chelsea is a dance instructor and choreographer for teens and children as young as 3. With the belief that her postive energy and happiness will in return create happiness in others, Chelsea strives to put a smile on every face in her path. She is the perfect addition to your next party or event!

What I do

Balloon Artist

Chelsea’s Balloons create a fun atmosphere for all ages. Children allow their imaginations to soar as the boys suddenly become pirates having a sword fight, and the girls climb their unicorns over a rainbow. Balloons have a way of bringing adults together too! A flower for your wife, a motorcycle for your boyfriend, or a crazy hat for grandpa... everyone can have fun with balloons!

Dancing

In addition to balloons and facepaint, Chelsea definitely knows how to get the party started with some dancing and games. Chelsea recieved her BFA in dance from Jacksonville University and began working as an instructor and choreographer at Shoreline Dance Academy after graduation.

Face Painter

Party guests of all ages will enjoy the art of face painting. Chelsea comes equip with two palettes of paints, creating beautiful designs on the cheek or over the eyes. Chelsea has taken all sorts of requests from her costomers. From Minecraft, to pennywise the clown, and even a professional wrestler, she will do her best to accommodate your special requests!

Craft Artisan

Looking for handmade and personalized gifts for friends and family? Chelsea creates art out of wine glasses, seashells, driftwood, and more! She can even create personalized party favors for your party guests!
